Our Mission!
Our mission is to spread kindness and bring people together with our community service Advent calendar. Every day, we’ll share simple challenges—like donating, volunteering, or doing something thoughtful to inspire generosity and celebrate the festive spirit. Together, let’s make Singapore a warmer, more connected place, one good deed at a time! We hope you enjoy our site!
