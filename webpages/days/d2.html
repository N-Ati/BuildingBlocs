Volunteer With Us!
Day 2

Spread some festive care to your loved ones too! Today’s
challenge invites you to call or visit an elderly relative or
family member to check on them and converse with them.

Here are some conversation starters from us: “Did you ever
celebrate Christmas growing up? What were some traditions
you did?”, “What is something you wish the younger
generation would understand better?”, “What is your
favorite festive food to eat or make?”
