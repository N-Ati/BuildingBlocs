Volunteer With Us!
Day 16

Keeping the public transport system up and running is no
easy feat in Singapore! Many people are involved in the
operations of the trains and buses we rely on daily for
commuting, especially during the festive season.

To show some gratitude to those who work on something so
essential in our lives, write a small thank you note and hand it
to your train ticketing centre staff, or bus driver!
